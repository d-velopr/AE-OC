Who We Are

Automotive Entertainment OC

At HB Automotive Entertainment, we merge cutting-edge technology with a passion for precision to deliver world-class automotive audio and customization solutions. Based in Huntington Beach, California, our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with CNC machining, laser scanning, and 3D printing capabilities—allowing us to create truly bespoke components tailored to the most exacting standards. Whether it’s enhancing acoustics or fabricating custom interiors, we’re driven by innovation at every step.

With a proven track record of custom installations in some of the world’s most elite vehicles—Bugatti, Ferrari, Aston Martin, McLaren, and beyond—we specialize in transforming high-performance machines into fully immersive, high-fidelity experiences. Serving Orange County and clients across the nation, HB Automotive Entertainment isn’t just about upgrades—it’s about elevating the art of automotive expression.

What We Do

Custom Craftsmanship and Installation

This isn’t plug and play. This is scan, design, cut, build—again and again until it fits like it was born there. CNC machines humming like symphonies. Lasers dancing with the surface. 3D prints coming to life like ghosts solidifying from air. It’s art. It’s math. It’s madness, tamed. Whether it’s a show-stopping audio system or a one-off console trim, HB doesn’t “install”—they engineer experiences. When the door shuts and the music hits, you’ll know it wasn’t done anywhere else.

Every project is a balance between precision and passion, where the smallest detail is treated like the biggest breakthrough. The result? Because in a world of mass production, true custom is the last frontier.
